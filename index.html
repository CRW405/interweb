Controls

Windows and Taskbar

If you dont want to look at a window then just click on the little "_" or "X" to get rid of it.

If you decide you actually did want that window then just find it on the taskbar below.

Clock

If you click the clock in the taskbar you can toggle between AM/PM format and 24 hour format

If the time on the clock is incorrect than it may be due to your browser's privacy settings.
